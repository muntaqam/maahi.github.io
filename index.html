Cheesecake recipe

3 easy steps to a delicious cheesecake

Serving size: 8

Ingredients:

  • cream cheese,softened(16oz.)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • crust
Directions:
  1. Heat oven to 325°F
  2. Beat cream cheese,sugar and vanilla with mixer until blended.Add eggs;beat just until blended.
  3. Pour into crust.
  4. Bake 40 minutes or until center is almost set.
  5. Cool.Refrigerate for 3 hours.
